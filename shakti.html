Degree Programmes We Offer

Admission to all the degree programmes shall be made through Competitive Entrance Examination conducted by Indian Council of Agricultural Research(ICAR).

Bachelor Degree Programmes

Candidate must have passed in 10+2 Examination either in PCB/PCMB/PCM/Inter-Agriculture

Master Degree Programmes

Candidate must have passed Bachelor degree examination from a recognized university in the concerned field under 10+2+4/10+6 years degree.

Doctoral Degree Programmes

Indian nationals having passed Master's Degree in the concerned subject from any recognized university in India and abroad.

About Us

Dr. Rajendra Prasad Central Agricultural University, formerly Rajendra Agricultural University, is a public central agriculture university
